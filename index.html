BerryCollector!

You will be given a random # at the start of the game.

There are 4 berries. By clicking on an item you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random #, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random #.

Try to figure out the value of each berry!

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each berry
