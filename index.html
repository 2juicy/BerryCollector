You will be given a random # at the start of the game.

There are 4 crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random #, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random #.

The value of each crystal in hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal
